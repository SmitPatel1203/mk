Pitrupaksha Shradh 2023: Honoring Departed Souls with Tarpan and Food Donations to Needy

Pitrupaksha is an important time to remember and honor departed loved ones by performing offerings on their tithi. In 2023, Pitrupaksha starts on 29th September and ends on 14th October. According to Brahmpurana, ancestors should be worshiped before the Gods as it pleases them. Shradh offerings can be made in the form of tarpan, food, and dakshina to Brahmins or the needy. During Pitrupaksha, it is customary to donate food to the needy to honor the departed soul with a cause. MK Foundation serves free, nutritious food to underprivileged families of hospital patients and children from distant places. You can also contribute to the cause by joining their food distribution drive during Pitrupaksha.
